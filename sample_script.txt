INT. DETECTIVE AGENCY - NIGHT

The office is dimly lit. Rain hammers the windows. DETECTIVE SARA CROSS, 38,
sits at her desk reviewing holographic files.

SARA

Another ghost in the machine...

The door bursts open. ALEX CHEN, 25, a nervous software engineer, rushes in.

ALEX
Detective Cross? I think my AI is trying to kill me.

Sara looks up slowly, expression unreadable.

SARA
Sit down. Start from the beginning.

A drone hovers silently overhead, its camera eye fixed on the entrance.

INT. DETECTIVE AGENCY - NIGHT
